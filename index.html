A Tribute to Nigerian youths

I wanted to write an eulogy about person i respect,but i have so many and most of them fall under the category of the Nigerian youths

Here is to us,I admire Nigerian youths.Here is a Tribute to every youthful Nigerian that is surviving.To those who are struggling to meet ends's meet.Life is hard but iferia mde it harder but here you are surviving.You have done well for yourself.
To our fallen heroes, to all the youths that are tired, to all who have suffered. To all who have given up! but we rise from the ashes. I acknowledge every single Nigerian youth who stands up against injustice. To thsoe who want no more police brutality, injustice, irrelevant polices
I admire your courage.To all who came out to make out voices heard. We have been in suffering for long

I applaud those who have lost a loved one due to the following:

  1. Police Brutality
  2. Injustice in the court system
  3. Poor transportation roads
  4. Corrupt Policies
You are strong, thank you for not giving up. We are grateful to those who were able to share their pain with the world and to those that are silent about it, we understand.If you are able to move on from pain, i commend you for your efforts.
Dear Nigerian youths,I admire your strenght and your quest for greatness, its our time and season. Here is to us,They will tell stories of us. The strenght of the Nigerian youths.Our Hope will not die We are almost there. We are the difference, we are the change
